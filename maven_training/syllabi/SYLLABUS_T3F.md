# COURSE SYLLABUS — T3-F: MSC FORCE MULTIPLIER
## Maven Smart System (MSS) — USAREUR-AF

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Level** | T3-F (MSC Force Multiplier — Unit Data Trainer Certification) |
| **Duration** | Half day (4 hours) |
| **Prerequisite** | TM-20, Builder (Go on file); unit commander nomination |
| **Audience** | MSC-level personnel designated for local TM-10 training sustainment: data NCOs, S6 staff, unit data stewards |
| **Format** | Instructor-led walkthrough + teach-back evaluation |
| **Location** | MSC site (typically delivered during MTT rotation) |

---

**BLUF:** T3-F certifies Unit Data Trainers (UDTs) to deliver TM-10 independently at their unit, proctor TM-10 exams, and report training status to C2DAO. Half-day course: learn the materials, prove you can deliver, get certified.

---

## Learning Objectives

| # | Objective |
|---|---|
| 1 | Navigate the TM-10 document set: course content, lesson plans, syllabus, exercises, exams, and T&EOs |
| 2 | Use the TM-10 lesson plan to deliver a block of instruction |
| 3 | Administer TM-10 pre-test and post-test: timing, answer key handling, scoring |
| 4 | Score a T&EO correctly: identify critical items, make Go/No-Go decisions |
| 5 | Deliver a TM-10 block to standard in front of an audience |

---

## Go Criteria

T3-F Go requires:
- **Teach-back:** satisfactory on technical accuracy (content is correct) **and** lesson plan adherence (follows the plan, uses check-on-learning questions)

T3-F Go authorizes the candidate to receive the UDT designation and begin delivering TM-10 independently.

---

## No-Go Remediation

| Outcome | Action |
|---|---|
| **No-Go — technical accuracy** | Candidate reviews TM-10 course content for the failed block; re-attempts teach-back at next T3-F iteration or scheduled remediation event |
| **No-Go — lesson plan adherence** | Candidate reviews lesson plan walkthrough procedures; re-attempts at remediation event |

---

## Key Tips

| Risk | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Lesson plan deviation | Follow the plan as written. If you think something is wrong, deliver as published and report to C2DAO after. |
| Evaluation assistance | The moment you help a trainee during evaluation, the evaluation is void. Announce the switch clearly and do not assist. |
| Classification block | Block 6 is the highest-stakes TM-10 block. Hard No-Go item. Walk through classification rules slowly. |
| Know your limits | If a request exceeds your authority, say so and connect with C2DAO. |

---

## Continuation

T3-F graduates serve as Unit Data Trainers. The path for UDTs who want to become full instructors:
1. Continue serving as UDT (build teaching experience)
2. Complete TM-30 if not already done
3. Seek C2DAO selection for T3-I (Instructor Certification)
4. Complete T3-I Phase 1 + Phase 2

T3-F is not a prerequisite for T3-I, but T3-F experience is valued in the selection process.
