| **Duration** | 3 days (24 hours) |

---

**BLUF:** T3-F certifies Unit Data Trainers (UDTs) to deliver TM-10 independently at their unit, facilitate TM-20 refresher labs (Blocks 1-10), proctor TM-10 exams as a standalone activity, and report training status to C2DAO. After this course you can sustain MSS basic training at your MSC between MTT visits.

## Practical Exercises

### Exercise 1: Teach-Back

| Parameter | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Block selection | Any TM-10 lesson block (candidate's choice; instructor may direct to avoid duplicates) |
| Audience | T3-F classmates (role-play as TM-10 trainees) |
| Materials | Candidate uses the published TM-10 lesson plan for the selected block |
| Duration | 15–20 minutes |
| Evaluation criteria | Technical accuracy (content is correct); materials use (follows lesson plan, uses check-on-learning questions) |

### Exercise 2: Go/No-Go Practicum

| Parameter | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Task | Administer 1 T&EO (instructor assigns from TM10-01 through TM10-06) to a role-player |
| Evaluation criteria | Correct scoring of all performance measures; correct overall Go/No-Go decision; clear mode transition announcement; no assistance during evaluation; proper documentation |

### Exercise 3: Troubleshooting Exam

| Parameter | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Issues | 3 pre-staged environment failures from the set of 5 common issues |
| Pass standard | Resolve 2 of 3 within the prescribed time limits |

## Go Criteria

T3-F Go requires **all three:**
1. Teach-back: satisfactory on technical accuracy **and** materials use
2. Go/No-Go practicum: correct overall Go/No-Go decision + proper documentation
3. Troubleshooting exam: resolve 2 of 3 issues

T3-F Go authorizes the candidate to receive the UDT designation and begin delivering TM-10 independently.

---

## No-Go Remediation

| Outcome | Action |
|---|---|
| **No-Go — teach-back (technical accuracy)** | Candidate reviews TM-10 course content for the failed block; re-attempts teach-back at a scheduled remediation event or next T3-F iteration |
| **No-Go — teach-back (materials use)** | Candidate reviews lesson plan walkthrough procedures; re-attempts at remediation event |
| **No-Go — Go/No-Go practicum** | Candidate reviews T&EO scoring procedures and attends an additional calibration session with C2DAO instructor; re-attempts at remediation event |
| **No-Go — troubleshooting exam (0 of 3)** | Candidate must attend next T3-F iteration |
| **No-Go — troubleshooting exam (1 of 3)** | Candidate reviews troubleshooting procedures; re-attempts the 2 failed issues at a scheduled remediation event |
| **No-Go — all three areas** | Candidate must attend next full T3-F iteration |

## Key Tips

| Risk | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Lesson plan deviation | Follow the plan as written. If you think something is wrong, deliver as published and report to C2DAO after. Do not freelance during delivery. |
| Evaluation assistance | The moment you help a trainee during the evaluation, the evaluation is void. Announce the switch to evaluation mode clearly and do not assist. |
| Classification block | Block 6 (classification and export) is the highest-stakes block in TM-10. It is a hard No-Go item on the T&EO. Walk through the classification rules slowly and check every trainee's first export. |
| Answer key security | Never distribute answer keys. Print student exam versions only. Keep the answer key in your instructor binder. |
| Know your limits | If a request exceeds your authority (TM-20 initial cert, TM-30+, enrollment exceptions, curriculum changes), say so and escalate to C2DAO. Attempting training beyond your scope is worse than saying no. |

## Continuation

T3-F graduates serve as Unit Data Trainers. There is no "T3-F Advanced" — the continuation path for UDTs who want to become full instructors is:
1. Continue serving as UDT (build teaching experience)
2. Complete TM-30 if not already done
3. Seek C2DAO selection for T3-I (Instructor Certification)
4. Complete T3-I Phase 1 + Phase 2

T3-F and T3-I are independent — T3-F is not a prerequisite for T3-I. However, T3-F experience is valuable for T3-I candidates and will be considered in the C2DAO selection process.
