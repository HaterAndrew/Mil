# TM-40 — Specialist & Warfighting Function Track Overview

**Prerequisite:** SL 3 (Advanced Builder) — required for all SL 4 tracks
**Leads to:** SL 5 series (selected specialist tracks only)

---

## Purpose

SL 4 is the third tier of the Maven Skill Set (MSS) training program. It branches into fourteen parallel tracks — six Warfighting Function (WFF) tracks and eight Specialist tracks — each tailored to a specific operational or technical role. Completion of SL 3 is required before enrolling in any SL 4 track.

---

## Track Overview

### Warfighting Function Tracks (A–F)

These tracks apply MSS capabilities to specific warfighting functions. They are role-specific and operationally focused.

| Track | Warfighting Function | Audience |
|-------|---------------------|----------|
| SL 4A | Intelligence | G2, S2, all-source analysts |
| SL 4B | Fires | FA officers, targeting teams |
| SL 4C | Movement & Maneuver | G3/S3, maneuver planners |
| SL 4D | Sustainment | G4/S4, logistics officers |
| SL 4E | Protection | Provost Marshal, protection cells |
| SL 4F | Mission Command | CPs, battle staff, commanders |

WFF tracks focus on applying data tools to mission planning, MDMP support, and operational analysis within their functional domain. Participants build operational dashboards, configure mission-relevant datasets, and integrate data products into their unit's planning cycle.

### Specialist Tracks (G–O)

These tracks develop technical depth for personnel who design, build, or manage data systems in support of Army operations.

| Track | Specialization | Audience |
|-------|---------------|----------|
| SL 4G | Operations Research/Systems Analysis (ORSA) | ORSAs, analysts |
| SL 4H | AI Engineer | AI developers, data engineers |
| SL 4M | ML Engineer | ML practitioners, model developers |
| SL 4J | Program Manager | PMs, product owners |
| SL 4K | Knowledge Manager | KMs, information managers |
| SL 4L | Software Engineer | SWEs, platform developers |
| SL 4N | UI/UX Designer | UX designers, product designers |
| SL 4O | Platform Engineer | Platform engineers, DevOps, infrastructure leads |

Specialist tracks require deeper engagement with Foundry platform capabilities: pipeline construction, ontology design, model integration, and application development.

**ASF Balanced Team Alignment.** SL 4N, SL 4J, and SL 4L form the Army Software Factory (ASF) "balanced team" triad — Designer, Product Manager, and Software Engineer. SL 4O complements this triad by providing the infrastructure and deployment pipeline expertise that enables balanced-team products to reach production. SL 4O also cross-references SL 4H (AI Engineer) for ML platform operations.

---

## Common Standards

All SL 4 tracks share the following completion criteria:

- Demonstrate proficiency in track-specific tasks using Maven Skill Set tools
- Build at least one functional data product (dashboard, pipeline, or application) relevant to the assigned warfighting function or specialty
- Pass the SL 4 post-assessment for the enrolled track
- Adhere to C2DAO and MSS governance standards established in SL 3

---

## Enrollment

Participants select one SL 4 track based on their duty position and career field. Dual enrollment is possible with G3 approval. See ENROLLMENT_SOP and POI_MSS for scheduling and seat allocation procedures.
