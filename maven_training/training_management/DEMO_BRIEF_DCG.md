## THE PROBLEM

- No organic MSS training capability exists at any echelon in the theater or in the Army.
- Palantir offers product training but does not cover data literacy, operational integration, or Army governance. It does not reach BDE/BN.
- ODT is absorbing the training mission by default, degrading its primary build and maintain mission.
- Partner nations operating in the USAREUR-AF MSS environment have requested training. No delivery mechanism exists to meet that demand.

## THE GAP

ODT built the curriculum, governance, and digital infrastructure. **No Mobile Training Team (MTT) exists** to travel to MSCs and deliver training at theater scale. Without an MTT, execution depends on individual unit initiative — creating inconsistent training quality and coverage gaps across the AOR. Partner nation training is impossible without a dedicated team.
