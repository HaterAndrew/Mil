# DOCTRINIZING MAVEN: A TRAINING CAPABILITY FOR CDA
**USAREUR-AF Operational Data Team | March 2026**

---

**BLUF:** The theater has a skills gap that undermines the Common Data Architecture. ODT built a complete, doctrine-backed training program (TRADOC-aligned governance, full draft publications). No organizational owner exists to deliver it at scale. Three COAs below. Inaction means the program dies while no one else builds one.

---

## THE PROBLEM

USAREUR-AF directed theater-wide MSS use as the data layer for the Common Data Architecture (CDA). The system is fielded. The workforce is not trained. ODT has been running bootcamps and classes, but the effort is unstructured and not scaling. Untrained personnel cannot use what they do not understand; without visible training investment, units treat MSS as optional.

## THE SOLUTION

ODT built a complete training program — 7 course levels, full draft publications, full governance package (MTP, POI, CAD, TEO, Enrollment SOP, FDP). Coalition-ready (NATO doctrine integrated). All courses use Army Task-Condition-Standard framework with Go/No-Go evaluations.

| Level | Course | Hours | Audience |
|-------|--------|-------|----------|
| TM-10 | Maven User | 8 | All personnel |
| TM-20 | Builder | 40 | Data-adjacent staff |
| TM-30 | Advanced Builder | 40 | Unit data leads |
| TM-40/50 | Specialist Tracks (20) | 40 ea | WFF analysts, engineers, PMs |
| FBC | Foundry Bootcamp | 5 days | TM-20+ w/ approved projects |
| TM-SL | Senior Leader | 1 day | O-5 / E-9+ executives |
| T3 | Instructor Pipeline | varies | Future instructors |

## THE RECOMMENDATION

Stand up dedicated MSS instructors. ODT built the program — someone else needs to deliver it. **COA 1 — 7ATC** assumes training; ODT as proponent. Leverages TRADOC infrastructure. Risk: buy-in, billets. **COA 2 — ODT** grows a training cell. Fastest standup. Risk: competes with core build mission. **COA 3 — G3/G37** assumes delivery. Risk: longest standup, depends on ODT for ramp. **Without dedicated instructors, the program stalls and MSS never gets into the hands of the NCOs.**
