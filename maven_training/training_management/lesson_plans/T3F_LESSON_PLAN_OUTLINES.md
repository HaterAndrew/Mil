### Block 1 — UDT Role, Authorities, and Limitations
**Duration:** 30 min | **Method:** Brief | **Time:** 0800–0830

**Purpose:** Set expectations. The UDT delivers SL 1 using published materials — nothing more, nothing less.

**TLO:** The candidate will state the UDT's authorities (deliver SL 1, administer evaluations, proctor exams, report status) and limitations (no SL 2+, no curriculum modification, no enrollment exceptions).

**Key Delivery Notes:**
- Walk through the authorities/limitations table from T3-F Course Content §1-4
- Emphasize: "You deliver SL 1. That's your lane. Everything else goes to C2DAO."
- Cover sustainment requirements: quarterly delivery, semi-annual PD, annual re-cert
- Explain the escalation principle: knowing your limits is a strength, not a weakness

**Assessment:** None — orientation block.

---

### Block 2 — TM-10 Materials Walkthrough
**Duration:** 60 min | **Method:** Walkthrough | **Time:** 0830–0930

**Purpose:** The UDT delivers published materials. This block ensures candidates know what every document is, where it lives, and how to use the lesson plan.

**TLO:** The candidate will locate each TM-10 document (course content, lesson plans, syllabus, exercises, exams, T&EOs), describe its purpose, and demonstrate how to use the lesson plan to prepare for a block.

**Key Delivery Notes:**
- Open each document in the repository and walk through its structure
- Focus on the lesson plans: show block structure (Purpose, TLO/ELOs, Instructor Notes, Student Activity, Check-on-Learning, Common Errors)
- Emphasize: "Follow the lesson plan. If you think something is wrong, deliver as written and report to C2DAO after."
- Cover pre-course checklist: account verification, workstation testing, materials printing, C2DAO notification
- Cover exam administration: timing, answer key security, scoring, documentation
- Cover reporting: Unit Training Status Report format, 5-duty-day timeline, No-Go 24-hour reporting

**Student Activity:** Each candidate opens each document independently; confirms they can navigate from lesson plan to exercise to T&EO for a TM-10 block.

**Assessment:** Formative — all candidates must independently locate all documents.

### Block 3 — Evaluation Procedures
**Duration:** 45 min | **Method:** Walkthrough | **Time:** 0945–1030

**Purpose:** UDTs administer Go/No-Go evaluations. This block covers how T&EOs work and how to score them.

**TLO:** The candidate will explain T&EO scoring, identify critical items in TM-10 T&EOs, and describe Go/No-Go determination and No-Go procedures.

**Key Delivery Notes:**
- Walk through a TM-10 T&EO: task, condition, standard, performance measures, critical items
- Critical items: classification marking (TM10-06), production environment avoidance
- Go determination: Go on all critical items + Go on required number of non-critical items
- Mode transition: announce the switch from instruction to evaluation clearly — "I cannot provide assistance"
- If you assist during evaluation, the evaluation is void
- No-Go procedures: same-day notification, specific deficiencies, documentation, remediation coordination, 24-hour report to C2DAO

**Student Activity:** Walk through a sample T&EO; identify critical items; discuss a borderline scenario.

**Assessment:** Formative — critical items must be correctly identified.

---

### Block 4 — Teach-Back (Evaluated)
**Duration:** 75 min | **Method:** Evaluation | **Time:** 1030–1145

**Purpose:** The teach-back is the Go/No-Go gate for T3-F.

**TLO:** The candidate will deliver a 10–15 minute SL 1 block demonstrating satisfactory technical accuracy and lesson plan adherence.

**Key Delivery Notes:**
- Each candidate delivers 1 SL 1 block (assigned by instructor to avoid duplicates)
- Other candidates role-play as SL 1 trainees
- Evaluator scores on: (1) Technical accuracy — content is correct; (2) Lesson plan adherence — follows the plan, uses check-on-learning questions
- Schedule ~15 minutes per candidate (10-15 delivery + brief feedback); 5 candidates = 75 min

**Student Activity:** Deliver the assigned block. Receive feedback.

**Assessment:** **EVALUATED.** Go: satisfactory on both technical accuracy and lesson plan adherence. No-Go: unsatisfactory on either.

---

### Block 5 — Certification and Closeout
**Duration:** 15 min | **Method:** Brief | **Time:** 1145–1200

**Purpose:** Administrative closeout; UDT designation; what to do when you get back to your unit.

**Key Delivery Notes:**
- Go/No-Go results
- UDT designation memorandum paperwork
- Sustainment review: quarterly delivery, semi-annual PD, annual re-cert
- Reporting templates: Unit Training Status Report, No-Go report
- C2DAO contact information
- "Schedule your first SL 1 within 30 days."

**Student Activity:** Complete designation paperwork. Receive templates and contacts.
