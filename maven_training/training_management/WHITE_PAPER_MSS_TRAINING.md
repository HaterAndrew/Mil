# THE STRATEGIC CASE FOR MSS TRAINING ACROSS USAREUR-AF
**USAREUR-AF Operational Data Team | March 2026**

---

## The Problem

MSS onboarding across USAREUR-AF is informal and ad hoc. The results: data quality degradation, governance violations, and decision-quality failures when commanders rely on products built by untrained personnel. The force is not uniformly qualified on MSS.

## The Operational Imperative

Data is a warfighting input. Every WFF — intelligence, fires, sustainment, maneuver, protection, mission command — depends on timely, accurate, well-governed data products. An untrained force cannot fully exploit MSS in competition or conflict. The time to build a qualified workforce is now, in garrison.

## The Solution

The USAREUR-AF MSS Training Program is a complete, doctrine-aligned curriculum using the Army's Task-Condition-Standard framework with Go/No-Go evaluations — not familiarization, not vendor training.

| Level | Course | Hours | Audience |
|-------|--------|-------|----------|
| SL 1 | Maven User | 8 hrs | All personnel — new Soldier in-processing |
| SL 2 | Builder | 40 hrs | Data-adjacent staff |
| SL 3 | Advanced Builder | 40 hrs | Unit data leads |
| SL 4A–F | WFF Functional | 40 hrs ea | G2/G3/G4/G5 functional staff |
| SL 4G–O | Technical Specialist | 40 hrs ea | ORSA, AI, ML, PM, KM, SWE, UX, PE |
| SL 5G–O | Advanced Specialist | 40 hrs ea | Senior technical staff |

254 training products are published. The portal is live. The governance framework is complete.

## Why an MTT

USAREUR-AF is geographically distributed. An MTT traveling to each MSC ensures every unit gets the same standard, the same evaluators, the same Go/No-Go — not the version one unit's data NCO learned from a colleague.

## What Theater-Wide Enactment Achieves

- **12 months:** All USAREUR-AF personnel SL 1 qualified
- **18 months:** All data-adjacent billets SL 3 qualified
- **Ongoing:** SL 4/5 specialist enrollment builds the technical bench
- **Measurable:** Monthly completion metrics by MSC, reported to G3

## The Ask

CG signature on TASKORD activating the MSS MTT: 4–6 billets, TDY-funded rotation to all MSCs, ODT as proponent, first cohort Q3 FY26.

The MTT will execute two missions at each MSC:
1. **Deliver SL 2, SL 3, and SL 4 courses** to the scheduled personnel
2. **Certify Unit Data Trainers (T3-F)** at each MSC, creating a local SL 1 delivery capability that sustains training between MTT visits

The instructor pipeline is formalized through T3-I (Instructor Certification), which replaces the previous ad-hoc apprenticeship model. T3-I establishes a three-tier instructor hierarchy (Instructor → Senior → Master) with documented criteria, authorities, and sustainment requirements. See Instructor Tier Definitions, Unit Data Trainer SOP, and MTT Operations SOP for implementation details.
