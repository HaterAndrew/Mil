## COMMANDER NOTIFICATION LETTER (SL 3 and above)

For SL 3 and above completions, C2DAO sends a notification letter to the trainee's commander.

```
DEPARTMENT OF THE ARMY
USAREUR-AF C2DAO
UNIT 29331
APO AE 09014

AETT-C2DAO                                     _________________ (Date)

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander, [Unit Name and Address]

SUBJECT: Maven Smart System Training Completion Notification

1. This memorandum notifies you that the following Soldier/Civilian under your
   command has successfully completed MSS training:

   Name:          ____________________________________________
   Rank/Grade:    ____________________________________________
   Course:        ____________________________________________
   Go Date:       ____________________________________________
   Evaluator:     ____________________________________________

2. This individual is now certified to perform the following functions on
   the USAREUR-AF Maven Smart System:

   _________________________________________________________________
   _________________________________________________________________

3. Certification is valid indefinitely, subject to sustainment training
   requirements specified in the MSS Mission Training Plan. Annual
   sustainment requirements include:

   - Annual SL 1 refresher (all levels)
   - Quarterly build exercises (SL 2 and above)
   - New feature familiarization training after major platform updates

4. Questions regarding this certification should be directed to the C2DAO
   Training POC via the unit MSS Administrator.

FOR THE C2DAO DIRECTOR:


________________________________
[C2DAO TRAINING OIC]
[Rank, Name]
Chief, C2DAO Training and Standards Division

```

## NO-GO NOTIFICATION LETTER

For No-Go results requiring commander notification (SL 4 series):

```
DEPARTMENT OF THE ARMY
USAREUR-AF C2DAO
UNIT 29331
APO AE 09014

AETT-C2DAO                                     _________________ (Date)

MEMORANDUM FOR Commander, [Unit Name and Address]

SUBJECT: Maven Smart System Training — No-Go Notification and
         Remediation Plan

1. This memorandum notifies you that the following Soldier/Civilian
   received a No-Go result on MSS specialist training:

   Name:          ____________________________________________
   Rank/Grade:    ____________________________________________
   Course:        ____________________________________________
   Evaluation Date: __________________________________________
   Evaluator:     ____________________________________________

2. Failed tasks and reasons are documented on the attached remediation
   form. A counseling has been completed.

3. Recommended remediation plan:
   - Remediation training hours required: ___________________
   - Scheduled re-evaluation date: _________________________
   - Recommended focus areas: ______________________________

4. The trainee may re-enroll for re-evaluation within 10 duty days of this
   notification. If a second No-Go is received, C2DAO approval is required
   before a third evaluation.

5. No-Go results do not constitute an adverse action or performance
   documentation. They document a training status requiring additional
   preparation. Most trainees who receive No-Go on initial evaluation
   pass re-evaluation following focused remediation.


________________________________
[C2DAO TRAINING OIC]
[Rank, Name]

ENCLOSURE: No-Go Remediation Form (CAD Appendix C)

```
