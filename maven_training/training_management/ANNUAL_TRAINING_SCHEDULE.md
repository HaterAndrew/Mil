## SCHEDULING PRINCIPLES

**1. Course Sequencing.** TM-10 is offered monthly; higher levels are offered quarterly or semi-annually depending on class size requirements and instructor availability. Plan in sequence — a unit cannot enroll personnel in TM-20 until TM-10 is complete.

**2. Access Provisioning Lead Time.** Access provisioning must begin at least as far in advance as the published lead times in the CAD. Units that submit enrollment requests without allowing sufficient provisioning lead time will not have access on Day 1. The training does not move for access issues.

**3. Quota Allocation.** Quotas are allocated by unit based on authorized strength and operational role. Unfilled quotas may be reallocated to other units at T-10 days (10 days prior to course start). Contact the C2DAO Training POC to check current quota availability.

**4. Training Freezes.** The training schedule will be suspended or reduced during major exercises (DEFENDER series, IRON WOLF, etc.) and during holiday block leave. Specific freeze dates are published in the USAREUR-AF training calendar by G3/G37. The MSS training schedule will be coordinated with the G3 calendar.

**5. Schedule Changes.** C2DAO will provide 30-day notice of any course cancellation. Units are expected to maintain a waitlist for each enrolled course so that cancelled seats can be rapidly refilled.

## UNIT PLANNING GUIDANCE

### Minimum Advance Planning Timeline

| Training Goal | Start Planning |
|---|---|
| TM-10 only | 2 weeks before desired course date |
| TM-10 → TM-20 | 6 weeks before TM-20 start (must complete TM-10 first) |
| TM-10 → TM-20 → TM-30 | 4 months before TM-30 start |
| TM-10 → TM-20 → TM-30 → TM-40A/B/C/F | 6 months before desired TM-40 completion |
| TM-10 → TM-20 → TM-40D/E | 3 months before TM-40D/E start |

### Newly Assigned Personnel (30-day TM-10 requirement)

A Soldier assigned on 1 April 2026 should be enrolled in TM10-2026-05 (7 April 2026) as the default path to meet the 30-day requirement. If that course is full, TM10-2026-06 (5 May) is the fallback. Units should maintain their own waitlist.

### Pre-Deployment Planning

Units preparing for deployment or major exercise should ensure all personnel complete TM-10 at minimum 60 days before deployment. Data team members (TM-20+) should be certified at least 90 days before deployment to allow for building and validating unit data products.
