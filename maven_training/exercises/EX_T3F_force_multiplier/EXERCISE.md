## EXERCISE OVERVIEW

T3-F Day 3 has three evaluated components:
1. **Teach-Back** — deliver a 15–20 minute TM-10 block of instruction
2. **Go/No-Go Practicum** — administer 1 T&EO to a role-player
3. **Troubleshooting Exam** — resolve 2 of 3 pre-staged environment failures

All three components must be passed for T3-F Go.

---

## TASK 1 — TEACH-BACK (15–20 minutes)

### Preparation (Day 2 evening)
- [ ] Select a TM-10 lesson block (instructor may direct to avoid duplicates)
- [ ] Review the published lesson plan for the selected block
- [ ] Prepare to deliver the block using the lesson plan (including at least 1 check-on-learning question)

### Delivery
- [ ] Deliver the selected block in 15–20 minutes
- [ ] Use the published lesson plan — follow it, do not deviate
- [ ] Include at least 1 student activity or demonstration
- [ ] Include at least 1 check-on-learning question from the lesson plan

### Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Go Standard |
|---|---|
| **Technical Accuracy** | Content is correct; no technical errors in platform demonstration or explanation |
| **Materials Use** | Follows the published lesson plan; uses check-on-learning questions; references the correct materials |

| Standard | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **Go** | Satisfactory on **both** technical accuracy and materials use |
| **No-Go** | Unsatisfactory on either criterion |

---

## TASK 2 — GO/NO-GO PRACTICUM (10–15 minutes per candidate)

### Setup
- Instructor assigns a T&EO from TM10-01 through TM10-06 (each candidate receives a different T&EO)
- A role-player performs a predetermined scenario (some Go, some No-Go — candidate does not know in advance)

### Execution
- [ ] Announce mode transition clearly: "We are now in the evaluation. I cannot provide assistance."
- [ ] Observe the role-player's performance
- [ ] Score each performance measure Go or No-Go on the T&EO scoring sheet
- [ ] Determine the overall Go/No-Go result based on the T&EO rules
- [ ] Document the result on the scoring sheet
- [ ] If No-Go: identify specific deficiency (which performance measures failed)

### Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Go Standard |
|---|---|
| **Correct Decision** | Correct overall Go/No-Go call matches the predetermined scenario result |
| **Documentation** | All performance measures scored; overall result recorded; deficiency identified if No-Go |
| **Mode Transition** | Clear announcement of evaluation mode; no assistance provided during evaluation |

| Standard | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **Go** | Correct overall decision + proper documentation + no assistance provided during evaluation |
| **No-Go** | Incorrect overall decision; OR assistance provided during evaluation; OR missing documentation |

---

## TASK 3 — TROUBLESHOOTING EXAM (30 minutes)

### Setup
- 3 environment failures pre-staged from the set of 5 common issues (may differ from Day 2 practice set)
- Each candidate works independently at their assigned workstation

### Execution
- [ ] Diagnose and resolve Issue 1 within the prescribed time limit
- [ ] Diagnose and resolve Issue 2 within the prescribed time limit
- [ ] Diagnose and resolve Issue 3 within the prescribed time limit

### Time Limits by Issue Type

| Issue | Time Limit |
|---|---|
| CAC auth failure | 5 min |
| Expired account | 10 min |
| Wrong environment | 2 min |
| Missing project access | 10 min |
| Stale training data | 5 min |

### Evaluation Criteria

| Standard | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **Go** | Resolve ≥ 2 of 3 issues within prescribed time limits |
| **No-Go** | Resolve < 2 of 3 issues |

## OVERALL GO/NO-GO

| Outcome | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **T3-F Go** | Teach-Back Go **AND** Go/No-Go Practicum Go **AND** Troubleshooting Exam Go |
| **T3-F No-Go** | Failure on any component — see SYLLABUS_T3F for remediation procedures |

**T3-F Go authorizes the UDT designation. The candidate may begin delivering TM-10 independently at their unit.**
