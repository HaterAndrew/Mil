## EXERCISE OVERVIEW

T3-I Phase 1 has two evaluated components on Day 5:
1. **Microteaching** — deliver a 20-minute block of instruction from TM-10, TM-20, or TM-30
2. **Written Exam** — 16 questions covering adult learning principles, T&EO structure, evaluation procedures, error management, and instructor performance standards

Both components must be passed for Phase 1 Go.

---

## TASK 1 — MICROTEACHING (20 minutes)

### Preparation (Day 4 evening)
- [ ] Select a lesson block from TM-10, TM-20, or TM-30 (instructor may direct to avoid duplicates)
- [ ] Review the published lesson plan for the selected block
- [ ] Prepare to deliver the block to the T3-I class (classmates role-play as trainees at the appropriate level)
- [ ] Plan at least 1 check-on-learning question and 1 student activity

### Delivery
- [ ] Deliver the selected block in exactly 20 minutes (instructor calls time)
- [ ] Use the published lesson plan — do not create your own materials
- [ ] Include at least 1 student activity (lab step, discussion question, or hands-on exercise)
- [ ] Include at least 1 check-on-learning question
- [ ] Demonstrate instructor circulation if the block includes a lab activity

### Evaluation Criteria

Scored on all 7 instructor observation criteria:

| # | Criterion | Go Standard |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Technical Accuracy** | Content is correct. No uncorrected errors. **CRITICAL — hard No-Go if unsatisfactory.** |
| 2 | **Instructional Clarity** | Explanations are clear and appropriately paced. Reinforced with operationally relevant examples. |
| 3 | **Student Engagement** | Students are actively engaged. Instructor identifies and assists students who are stuck. |
| 4 | **Check on Learning** | At least 1 check-on-learning question used. Questions require more than yes/no. |
| 5 | **Lab Management** | If block includes lab: lab runs on schedule. Instructor manages errors without solving for students. |
| 6 | **Evaluation Fidelity** | If block includes evaluation: T&EO procedures followed. No assistance during eval. **CRITICAL — hard No-Go if unsatisfactory.** |
| 7 | **Course Materials Currency** | Lesson plan is current. References correct platform version. |

| Standard | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **Go** | Satisfactory on ≥ 5 of 7 criteria; no unsatisfactory on Technical Accuracy (criterion 1) or Evaluation Fidelity (criterion 6) |
| **No-Go** | Satisfactory on < 5 of 7 criteria; OR unsatisfactory on Technical Accuracy; OR unsatisfactory on Evaluation Fidelity |

## TASK 2 — WRITTEN EXAM (60 minutes)

### Parameters
- [ ] 16 questions (50 points; multiple choice and short answer)
- [ ] Closed book, closed notes
- [ ] 60-minute time limit
- [ ] Passing score: 80% (40 of 50)

### Topics Covered
| Topic | Approximate Questions |
|---|---|
| Adult learning principles (Knowles, Kolb, scaffolding) | 4 |
| TCS framework and TLO/ELO hierarchy | 3 |
| T&EO structure and Go/No-Go procedures | 4 |
| Evaluation calibration and evaluator standards | 3 |
| Common trainee error management | 2 |
| Instructor performance standards (7 criteria) | 2 |
| Pre-course administration and exam procedures | 2 |

| Standard | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **Go** | ≥ 80% (40 of 50 points) |
| **No-Go** | < 80%; one re-take authorized within 10 duty days |

---

## OVERALL GO/NO-GO

| Outcome | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Phase 1 Go** | Microteaching Go **AND** Written Exam Go |
| **Phase 1 No-Go** | Failure on either component — see SYLLABUS_T3I for remediation procedures |

**Phase 1 Go authorizes progression to Phase 2 (Supervised Practicum). It does NOT authorize independent instruction.**
