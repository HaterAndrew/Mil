# Achieving Decision Dominance: The Arduous Pursuit of Operationalized Data

**Author:** Capt. Alexander K. Adkins, U.S. Army
**Publication:** *Military Review* 105, no. 1 (January-February 2025): 88
**Source:** Army University Press

---

## Key Concepts

### Operationalized Data
Data analyzed and presented in a way to be immediately actionable by those who consume it. Made available through the COP and fighting products.

### Automated Fighting Products (AFP)
- Commonly accessible staff/leader data visualization tools
- Supported by automated data pipelines
- Connected to live, authoritative data sources
- Reduce time required to produce staff running estimates
- Inform commander decision-making
- Platform-agnostic: only requires accessible platform, live data connection, visual display

### Decision Optimization Team (DOT)
Formal team at every echelon (division through HQDA) responsible for rapidly providing operationalized data through integration, analysis, and visualization of live authoritative data. Key tasks span data engineering, data science, and decision science. DOTs are "the operational arm of the Army's Unified Data Reference Architecture."

### DOT at Echelon
- **Division:** Consumers of AFPs in combat; produce minor formation-specific addendums; implement validated AI/ML tools
- **Corps:** Develop and deploy AFPs tailored to mission; produce theater-agnostic AFP suites scaling to company level; first triage of reach-back capability
- **ASCC:** Produce theater-specific COP; provide reach-back to corps; optimal echelon for AI/ML model training/validation. **Adkins explicitly names Maven Smart System as the USCENTCOM ASCC-level COP platform.**
- **HQDA:** Synchronize decision optimization within operations/resourcing cycles; governance for reach-back system; gateway to strategic development resources

### DOT Manning (FA Workforce)
- **FA 26B** (Information Network Engineer): Data engineering
- **FA 49** (ORSA): Data science, analytics, visualization, transformation leadership
- **FA 57** (Simulation Operations): Decision science, KM, simulation support, COP construction

### Training Model
- Gunnery table model (IWTS/MITS methodology) for DOT certification
- Individual → team → staff collective training progression
- Proposed "Algorithmic Warfare Master Gunner" course (5-week crawl-walk-run)
- 4-phase implementation over 5-year horizon: Reorganize/pilot → Training/doctrine revision → Expansion → Validation

### XVIII ABC Reference
Adkins notes: "The XVIII Airborne Corps Office of Data Transformation piloted the combination of the FA 26B, FA 49, and FA 57 efforts within their headquarters in a targeted effort to improve the data literacy of the corps, division, and direct reporting unit staffs to great effect, implementing novel data literacy and advanced command-and-control information systems training courses."

### Cost of Inaction
"Without a programmatic approach to achieving decision dominance, tactical commanders will continue the current asynchronous approach to innovate methods to achieve local decision dominance. That approach during large-scale combat operations will levy unrealistic expectations on tactical units while in combat as strategic resources will be underutilized at the point of need."
