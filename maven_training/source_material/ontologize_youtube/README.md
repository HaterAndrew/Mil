# Ontologize YouTube Channel — Reference Index

## Channel Overview

**Channel:** [@Ontologize](https://www.youtube.com/@Ontologize)
**Organization:** Ontologize — official Palantir training partner
**Focus:** Palantir Foundry and AIP platform training
**Instructors:** Gena (primary), Ben (secondary) — former Palantir engineers (team includes Palantir's former global head of training and the #1 ranked StackOverflow contributor for Foundry)
**Clients:** PwC, Conagra Brands, Swiss Re, EY, U.S. Air Force, U.S. DoD
**Website:** [ontologize.com](https://ontologize.com/)

The channel provides hands-on video walkthroughs of Palantir Foundry features, typically building alongside official learn.palantir.com courses or demonstrating specific platform capabilities. Videos range from 4–67 minutes and cover data connection, pipeline building, ontology design, Workshop applications, AIP Logic, AIP Agents, RAG workflows, ML operations, and OSDK development.
