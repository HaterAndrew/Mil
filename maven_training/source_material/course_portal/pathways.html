T3 — Train-the-Trainer Pathways

T3 tracks sit outside the main TM-10 → TM-50 chain. They certify personnel to deliver training rather than consume it. Each has its own prerequisite gate and selection process.

T3-I — Instructor Certification

Certified MSS Instructor

Transforms qualified operators into certified instructors. Covers adult learning principles, platform deep-dive for instruction, lab facilitation, assessment design, Go/No-Go standardization, and common trainee error management. Graduates enter a supervised practicum before independent teaching authorization.

TM-30 (prereq) T3-I Phase 1: Course T3-I Phase 2: Practicum
Gate: TM-30 Advanced Builder (Go on file) + C2DAO Training OIC selection

T3-F — MSC Force Multiplier

Unit Data Trainer (UDT)

Creates a sustainment training capability at the MSC level. Graduates earn the UDT designation and are authorized to deliver TM-10 independently, facilitate TM-20 refresher labs, proctor exams, and report training status to C2DAO. Does not replace C2DAO instructor certification.

TM-20 (prereq) T3-F Course UDT Designation
Gate: TM-20 Builder (Go on file) + Unit Commander nomination
