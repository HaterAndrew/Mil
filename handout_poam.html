Risk Assessment
Risk of Inaction
Without an organizational owner, the training program stalls. ODT burns out absorbing the training mission by default. The Army's first organic MSS training capability stops advancing while no one else is building one.
Corps-Level Precedent
XVIII Airborne Corps piloted a similar model at Corps level. Their ODTs—multifunctional teams of PMs, designers, engineers, and data scientists—delivered a BDA visualization prototype in 3 months, MVP in 6, POR handoff in 9. SEC ARMY directed ODT experimentation under TiC 2.0. MSS training tracks (TM-40J–O) produce the skill sets this model requires. USAREUR-AF adapts these lessons to theater-level requirements under our own organizational structure. —Forney et al., “Fighting with Live Data,” Military Review, Feb 2026
